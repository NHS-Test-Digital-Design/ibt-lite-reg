Use the camera on your phone or computer to scan the Royal Mail barcode in the home test kit.

This appears on the right-hand side of your label. Do not scan the QR code, which is on the left.

The Royal Mail barcode is also different from your home test kit barcode.

You can also manually enter the 13 character reference below the barcode.
